Research interests

Topological complex-analytic geometry, quantum computing, translation & accessibility, FOSS, and birds.

What I'm doing

I have just defended my thesis on the subject of twisting cochains in complex geometry and Chern classes of coherent analytic sheaves at the Université d'Aix-Marseille, and am currently working as part of the DerSympApp project at the Université de Montpellier.

I also work on translating various mathematical articles; see thosgood.com/translations.
