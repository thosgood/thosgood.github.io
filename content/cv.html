Research

Current research. (a) Topological and simplicial methods in complex-analytic geometry; coherent analytic sheaves; holomorphic Deligne cohomology. (b) Language-independent approaches to written mathematics; formal translation via a categorical framework.

Research interests. Homotopical methods in complex geometry, (higher) category theory, translation & accessibility. (MSC2020, in numerical order: 14F08, 14F43, 18F20, 18N60, 32C35, 32L05).
