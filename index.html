Research interests

Topological complex-analytic geometry, homotopical structures, quantum computing, translation & accessibility, FOSS, and birds.

What I'm doing

I am working on translating various mathematical articles; see thosgood.com/translations and thosgood.com/maths-dictionary.

What I've done

In the summer of 2020, I defended my PhD thesis at the Université d'Aix-Marseille on the subject of twisting cochains in complex geometry and Chern classes of coherent analytic sheaves. After that I worked as part of the DerSympApp project at the Université de Montpellier.

I graduated with an MMath (Mathematics) from the University of Oxford in 2016, after having finished my BA in Mathematics there. Whilst there I wrote two theses.
